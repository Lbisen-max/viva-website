About Us

A sourcing partner built on transparency and consistency

Viva Global Commerce is an Indian export company committed to supplying high-quality agricultural, forest, handcrafted, and natural products to international buyers. We are building long-term relationships through quality products, transparent communication, ethical business practices, and reliable global logistics.

Based in Balaghat, Madhya Pradesh — a region close to India's agricultural and forest belts — we work directly with growers and artisans to keep our supply chain honest and our pricing competitive. Mahua, in particular, is a local specialty of our home region — giving us close, direct access that few exporters can match. Our vision is straightforward: become a trusted global sourcing partner, one shipment at a time.
